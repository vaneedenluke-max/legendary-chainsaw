Know the City

A few of our favourite neighbourhoods

Christchurch has distinct suburbs with very different characters. Here's a snapshot — we'll go deeper when we talk.

Merivale

Tree-lined streets, boutique shopping, excellent cafés. Established, leafy, and close to everything.

Cashmere

Elevated hillside living with views over the city and quick access to the Port Hills. Sought-after for families.

Sumner

Beachside village feel, 15 minutes from the city. A community of its own with a fiercely loyal following.

Fendalton

Some of Christchurch's finest homes, close to top schools and the Botanic Gardens. Quiet and prestigious.
