Our Story

Born from a love
of this city

Ōtautahi Concierge was founded in the heart of Christchurch's rebuild — a belief that this city deserved a world-class lifestyle service as bold and creative as its transformation.

We are local experts with global standards. Our team has deep roots in Christchurch — relationships with the best restaurants, hoteliers, event spaces, and service providers built over a decade. We don't just know this city; we love it.

Whether you're a resident seeking more time in your day, an executive visiting for business, or a family relocating from overseas — we bring calm, capability, and discretion to every request.

  • Discretion and confidentiality guaranteed
  • No request too large or too small
  • Deep network across Canterbury region
